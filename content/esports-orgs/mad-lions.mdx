MAD Lions was an international esports organization founded in 2017, primarily Spain-anchored. The 2021 LEC Spring and Summer championships were the org's signature competitive results, and the 2021 Worlds quarterfinal appearance gave the org an international result that few LEC brands of the era achieved.

The dissolution in 2025 was part of the post-LEC franchising shakeout, which removed several historical brands from the league as the franchising economics adjusted. The closure ended one of the recognized LEC brands of the early franchising era.

Status: dissolved. Profile preserved for historical depth alongside CLG and Team 3D.
