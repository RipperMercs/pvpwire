FaZe Clan is a North American esports organization and content brand founded in 2010. The esports division was established in 2016 and has built tier-1 rosters across Counter-Strike, Call of Duty, Apex Legends, Rainbow Six Siege, and Halo across its history.

The 2022 PGL Major Antwerp win in CS:GO is the signature competitive result. The international roster (karrigan, Twistzz, ropz, broky, rain) won the first post-COVID CS Major and the 2022 IEM Katowice, becoming the first non-Astralis roster to win an Intel Grand Slam.

FaZe trades on visibility and crossover brand value as much as competitive results. The Call of Duty operation runs in the CDL, and the broader content arm gives the org mainstream-press recognition beyond the strict esports audience. Business turbulence in 2023 and 2024 resulted in delisting and acquisition, but the esports operation has continued.
