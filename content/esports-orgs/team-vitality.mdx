Team Vitality is the leading French esports organization. Founded in 2013, the org has built its CS reputation around ZywOo, who joined in 2018 and remains one of the most decorated competitive Counter-Strike players of the modern era.

The 2024 PGL Major Copenhagen win, the first CS2 Major after the engine transition from CS:GO, is the org's signature competitive moment. The roster has continued to perform at tier-1 across the post-Major calendar.

The League of Legends operation runs in the LEC, the Rocket League roster has competed in RLCS, and the Valorant team competes in VCT EMEA. Brand visibility in EU is anchored by the French Twitch and YouTube market, where Vitality is a recognized name beyond pure competitive results.
