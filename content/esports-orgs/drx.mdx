DRX is a Korean esports organization founded in 2012 (under prior brand names including Korean Telecom variants and Kingzone DragonX, rebranded to DRX in 2020). The 2022 League of Legends World Championship win in San Francisco is the org's defining moment, with Deft completing his decade-long career by claiming the title in his final international run.

DRX entered Worlds 2022 as the lowest-seeded LCK team to ever win the championship, defeating T1 in the final. The storyline (Deft and Faker, longtime rivals from the early LCK era, meeting in a Worlds final with Deft winning) is among the most-cited single moments in LoL competitive history.

The org also operates in Valorant (VCT Pacific) and Apex Legends. The 2024 LCK relegation and re-promotion arc is part of the post-Worlds rebuild context.
