Paper Rex is a Singapore-based Valorant organization founded in 2020. The 2024 VCT Masters Madrid win is the org's signature international result and the SEA region's signature Valorant breakthrough.

The roster (f0rsakeN, mindfreak, Jinggg, something, d4v41) became the most-cited SEA Valorant lineup in the title's history. f0rsakeN and Jinggg are particularly recognized as among the top duelists in the international scene.

Paper Rex represents SEA at international Valorant events. The org's competitive identity is the aggressive, fast-paced default style associated with Pacific-region Valorant.
