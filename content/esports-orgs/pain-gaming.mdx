paiN Gaming is the longest-running tier-1 Brazilian esports organization. Founded in 2010, the org has fielded competitive rosters across League of Legends (CBLOL), Counter-Strike, Rainbow Six Siege, FIFA / EA Sports FC, and other titles across more than 15 years of continuous operation.

The LoL operation is the most-cited part of the brand: paiN has been a CBLOL perennial contender, with multiple split championships and Worlds qualifications across the LATAM-LoL bracket evolution.

The org is the broader LATAM regional anchor for esports brand recognition alongside Furia (CS-focused), LOUD (Valorant-focused), and MIBR (CS-focused).
