ENCE is a Finnish Counter-Strike organization founded in 2013. The 2019 IEM Katowice Major finals run is the org's signature competitive result and one of the most-cited Finnish-roster runs in CS history.

The roster has gone through multiple lineup cycles since the 2019 finals appearance. Currently in a rebuilding phase within the European tier-2 to tier-1 CS cycle.

ENCE is the most-recognized Finnish CS brand and adds Nordic-region depth to the orgs index alongside Astralis, Heroic, and the Norwegian and Swedish CS scene.
