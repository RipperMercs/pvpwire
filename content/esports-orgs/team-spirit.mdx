Team Spirit is a Russian-rooted tier-1 international esports organization. Founded in 2014, the org has held tier-1 rosters in Counter-Strike, Dota 2, and Apex Legends.

The 2021 TI10 win in Bucharest is the org's signature international result and one of the largest prize pools in esports history. The CS2 roster has been a top-three international team consistently across 2024 and 2025, with multiple Major podiums and the 2024 PGL Major Copenhagen final as the most-cited recent CS result.

The roster (donk, magixx, chopper, sh1ro, zont1x) is among the youngest tier-1 CS lineups, with donk widely cited as one of the best individual riflers in modern Counter-Strike.
