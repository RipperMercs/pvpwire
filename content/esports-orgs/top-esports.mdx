Top Esports, commonly referred to as TES, is a Chinese League of Legends organization founded in 2017. The org has been a multi-time LPL champion across the 2019 to 2024 window with the 2020 MSI runner-up finish as the signature international result.

The roster has historically been a star-anchored lineup with players including JackeyLove, Knight (former), and 369 across different competitive cycles. TES is one of the load-bearing CN LoL brands alongside BLG, JDG, EDG, and LNG.
