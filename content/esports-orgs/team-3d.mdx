Team 3D was one of the dominant North American Counter-Strike organizations during the early professional CS era. Founded in 2002, the roster produced results across the CPL circuit and the original esports league structures, with the 2005 CPL World Tour Championship as the signature result.

The organization is a benchmark for North American competitive Counter-Strike during the period when the game's professional infrastructure was being built. Team 3D dissolved in 2007 alongside the collapse of the CPL, which had supported the original NA pro CS scene.

Status: dissolved. Profile preserved for historical depth in the esports orgs index.
