OpTic Gaming is the most recognized Call of Duty organization in esports history. Founded in 2006, the org has fielded competitive CoD rosters across every major iteration of the franchise's competitive infrastructure: the original tournament circuit, MLG, the Call of Duty World League, and the modern Call of Duty League franchise era.

The 2017 Call of Duty Championship win is the org's signature competitive result. OpTic Texas (the CDL franchise) continues the brand identity in the modern CDL with the org back under the original OpTic ownership group after a few cycles of corporate restructuring.

The "GreenWall" supporter culture is one of the most-cited fan communities in NA esports and is a major part of the org's identity beyond pure competitive results. Also fields Valorant and Apex Legends rosters.
