Bilibili Gaming, commonly referred to as BLG, is a Chinese tier-1 esports organization founded in 2017 by the Bilibili streaming platform. The organization has been an LPL perennial contender across 2023 to 2025 with multiple split championships and a 2023 League of Legends World Championship finals appearance.

The roster has been built around Bin in the top lane and Knight in mid, with the broader supporting cast competing for international tier-1 recognition. The 2024 LPL Spring and Summer titles re-established BLG at the top of the LPL after the 2023 Worlds runner-up run.

Distinct from the parent Bilibili streaming platform: the org is the competitive operation, named after the broadcaster ownership.
