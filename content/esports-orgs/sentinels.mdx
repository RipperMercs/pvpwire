Sentinels is one of the recognized VCT Americas anchor organizations in Valorant. The 2021 win at VCT Masters Reykjavik, the first major international Valorant LAN, established the organization at the founding moment of the title's competitive history. The 2024 Masters Madrid win marked a return to top tier after a multi-season rebuild and reestablished the Sentinels brand at the international tier.

TenZ has been the throughline of multiple Sentinels eras and is among the most cited individual names in Valorant's pro history. The org also fields an Apex Legends roster competing in ALGS.
