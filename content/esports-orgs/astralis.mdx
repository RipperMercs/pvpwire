Astralis is a Danish CS organization whose 2018 to 2019 dominance produced one of the most cited dynasty runs in esports history. The four Majors won across 18 months (ELEAGUE Boston, FACEIT London, IEM Katowice, StarLadder Berlin) plus back-to-back Intel Grand Slams set a competitive bar that no CS roster has matched since.

The roster of dev1ce, gla1ve, dupreeh, Xyp9x, and Magisk became the reference point for tactical, set-piece Counter-Strike. The signature default-to-execute timings, the support-role economy, and the in-game leadership template are still cited by modern CS coaches.

Current operations are CS2-only, with the roster in a multi-year rebuild after the original Astralis Era core moved on. The brand equity from the dynasty run keeps the org at tier-1 even when results lag.
