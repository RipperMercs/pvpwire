SK Gaming is one of the oldest still-active esports organizations in the world. Founded in 1997 in Cologne as Schroet Kommando, the org predates "esports" as a term in its modern usage and has continuously operated competitive rosters across more than 25 years of esports history.

The Quake era through the early Counter-Strike era through the modern multi-game cycle are all part of the org's continuous timeline. SK has produced or fielded some of the most-cited names across multiple competitive scenes.

Currently competitive in CS2, League of Legends (LEC), FIFA / EA Sports FC, and Rainbow Six Siege. SK Gaming anchors the historical-depth dimension of the orgs index alongside Evil Geniuses (founded 1999) as the only two pre-2000 orgs still actively competing at tier-1.
