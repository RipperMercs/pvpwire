TSM, originally Team SoloMid, is one of the foundational North American League of Legends brands. Founded in 2009 by Reginald, the org was a multi-time LCS champion across the LCS franchise era and was historically the most-recognized NA LoL identity alongside Cloud9.

Current operations are in transition after a 2023 to 2024 restructuring. The org continues to field rosters in fighting games (Hungrybox via the Liquid roster historically; current FGC players), Apex Legends, and other titles, with the LoL operation reduced from its franchise-era scale.

TSM's brand recognition in NA esports remains tier-1 even with reduced league presence. Profile reflects the historic NA significance plus the current transition state.
