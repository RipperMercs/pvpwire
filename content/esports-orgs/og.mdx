OG is the only organization to win back-to-back The International championships in Dota 2, with TI8 in 2018 and TI9 in 2019. The TI8 win remains one of the most-cited underdog stories in esports history: the original roster left weeks before the event, and the assembled replacement lineup (with N0tail, JerAx, ana, Topson, Ceb) won the title.

The TI9 repeat in Shanghai a year later cemented the organization in Dota 2 history and made the original five-stack one of the most decorated competitive lineups in any esport.

Current operations have expanded into Counter-Strike, Rainbow Six Siege, and Valorant alongside the continuing Dota 2 roster.
