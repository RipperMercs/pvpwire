JD Gaming, commonly referred to as JDG, is a Chinese League of Legends organization founded in 2017 by JD.com. The org has been LPL perennial top-tier across 2023 to 2025, with the 2023 Mid-Season Invitational title in London as the signature international result.

The 2023 MSI run is one of the most-cited recent CN LoL titles. The roster (with 369, Kanavi, knight, Ruler, and Missing) was widely considered the strongest LPL lineup of the year. The 2023 Worlds run ended in disappointment, but the org's overall 2023 to 2025 stretch is among the most consistent in the LPL.
