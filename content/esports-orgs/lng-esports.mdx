LNG Esports is a Chinese League of Legends organization founded in 2019. The org has been an LPL playoff perennial across 2022 to 2025 with consistent Worlds qualifications.

The roster has been built around longstanding LPL stars at multiple positions. LNG provides additional CN representation in the international LoL tier alongside BLG, JDG, TES, and EDG.
