NRG Esports is a North American multi-game organization founded in 2015. The org has held tier-1 rosters across Counter-Strike, Apex Legends, Rocket League, Valorant, Halo, and Call of Duty.

The 2019 RLCS Season 7 World Championship in Rocket League is the org's signature international title. The Apex operation has produced multiple ALGS Championship deep runs across the title's competitive cycles, and the CS roster (acquired from ENCE) brought a tier-1 European core to the NA brand.

Mainstream-press visibility from celebrity ownership including Shaquille O'Neal, Jennifer Lopez, Marshawn Lynch, and Michael Strahan distinguishes NRG from most pure-esports orgs and gives the brand cross-audience reach beyond the competitive scene.
