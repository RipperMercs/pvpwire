KT Rolster is the longest-running Korean League of Legends organization still active. Founded in 2008 by KT Corporation, the org has been continuously operating in the LCK across the entire history of the league.

The KT versus SKT (now T1) rivalry was the dominant LCK matchup of the league's first decade and is the telecom-corporate-rivalry parallel to the broader Korean telecom market. The two orgs have met in playoff series across nearly every LCK split.

KT has produced multiple LCK split championships across its history. Current operations remain in the LCK; the org's identity is the long-tenured institutional presence rather than a single dynasty stretch.
