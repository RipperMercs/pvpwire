Gen.G is a tier-1 multi-game esports organization with operations in South Korea, North America, and China. Founded in 2017, the org has built tier-1 rosters across League of Legends (LCK), Valorant (VCT Pacific franchise), PUBG, Overwatch, and Apex Legends.

The 2024 VCT Champions win in Seoul is the org's signature international Valorant result. The LoL operation has been LCK perennial top-tier across 2024 to 2025 with multiple split championships and Worlds finalist appearances.

Gen.G is also one of the most institutionally professional esports orgs, with an organizational structure (Gen.G Academy in LA, the Esports Academy in Korea) that operates more like a traditional sports franchise than the typical esports brand.
