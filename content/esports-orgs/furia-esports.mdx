Furia Esports is the leading Brazilian Counter-Strike organization. Founded in 2017 in Sao Paulo, the org has been a perennial top-10 international CS team across the late CS:GO and CS2 eras, with multiple deep playoff runs at international Majors and tier-1 events.

The competitive identity is aggressive default play built around KSCERATO and yuurih, two of the most-cited Brazilian CS players of the modern era. The org's roster has been one of the most stable top-15 lineups in CS for the 2022 to 2025 window.

Brazil is the second-largest CS audience after Russia and the largest LATAM esports market broadly, and Furia carries the regional flag at international tournaments. The org also fields a Valorant roster in VCT Americas and an Apex Legends roster.
