Evil Geniuses is one of the oldest still-active North American esports brands. Founded in 1999, the organization has fielded competitive rosters across StarCraft, Dota 2, Counter-Strike, Valorant, and other titles across more than two decades.

The 2015 The International win in Dota 2 is one of the most-cited TI runs in the title's history. The 2023 VCT Champions win in Los Angeles, with a roster that surprised the seeded brackets, gave the org a second genre-defining international title eight years later.

The org went through significant turmoil in 2023 to 2024 (CS roster issues, broader restructuring) and the current 2025 to 2026 operation is rebuilding around the Valorant brand.
