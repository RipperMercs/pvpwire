Karmine Corp, commonly referred to as KCorp, is a French esports organization founded in 2020 by content creator Kameto. The org has built one of the most-visible esports crowd brands in modern Europe, frequently described as the "European football club" of esports for the size and intensity of its supporter culture.

Within four years of founding, Karmine entered the LEC (League of Legends EMEA Championship) franchise, secured a VCT EMEA Valorant slot, and competed in RLCS Rocket League. The franchise entries were unusual for a brand this young, driven by the org's massive content and audience footprint.

The competitive results have been mixed across the early seasons; the brand identity is the unique part of the operation. Karmine's home events at Paris venues regularly draw the largest French esports live crowds and represent a different commercial model than the typical tier-1 esports org.
