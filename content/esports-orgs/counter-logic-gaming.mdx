Counter Logic Gaming, commonly referred to as CLG, was one of the foundational North American League of Legends brands of the LCS era. Founded in 2010 by HotshotGG, the org was a tier-1 NA LoL identity throughout the 2011 to 2018 stretch.

The 2015 to 2016 stretch produced back-to-back LCS Summer and Spring championships and the 2016 MSI final appearance, the org's signature competitive era. Doublelift, Aphromoo, ZionSpartan, Pobelter, and Xmithie were the recognized 2016 lineup.

CLG dissolved in 2024 alongside the broader LCS franchise restructuring shakeout. The closure marked the end of one of the original NA LoL brands and is one of the more cited org-dissolutions of the post-franchising era.
