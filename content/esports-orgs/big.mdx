BIG, short for Berlin International Gaming, is a German Counter-Strike organization founded in 2017. The org is the longest-running modern German CS brand alongside MOUZ.

BIG has been a tier-1 EU CS contender across the 2018 to 2025 window with multiple international event finals appearances. The roster identity is built around methodical, tactical CS play with strong CT-side defaults.

The org represents the German CS scene at international events alongside MOUZ as the two recognized German CS brands of the modern era.
