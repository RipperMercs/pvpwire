MIBR, short for Made in Brazil, is a classic Brazilian Counter-Strike organization. Originally founded in 2003, the brand has gone through multiple rebranding eras across the CS 1.6, CS:GO, and CS2 cycles, with different roster compositions across each era.

The brand is one of the foundational Brazilian CS identities, with historical lineage tied to the dominant BR CS rosters of the late 2000s and the modern Furia era. Currently fielding a CS2 roster competing in international qualifiers and tier-2 events.

Profile clarifies the multiple rebranding eras for editorial accuracy. The current MIBR is the active legal entity continuing the brand identity.
