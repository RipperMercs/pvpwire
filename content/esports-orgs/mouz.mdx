MOUZ, formerly mousesports, is one of the oldest still-active CS organizations in continuous operation. Founded in Germany in 2002, the org has held tier-1 Counter-Strike rosters across CS 1.6, Source, Global Offensive, and CS2.

The 2024 to 2025 cycle saw the org return to deep Major runs after a multi-year rebuild, with a young CS2 roster centered on Jimpphat and torzsi competing for international titles. The org also fields a Rainbow Six Siege roster.

The MOUZ brand is heavily CS-skewed, which is unusual for tier-1 modern orgs (most run multi-game portfolios) but reflects the org's identity as a Counter-Strike specialist with two-decade brand continuity.
