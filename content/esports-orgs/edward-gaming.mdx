Edward Gaming, commonly referred to as EDG, is a Chinese League of Legends organization founded in 2013. The 2021 LoL World Championship win in Reykjavik is the org's defining moment, breaking a multi-year LCK dominance streak with a 3-2 final win over DWG KIA.

The 2021 roster (Flandre, JieJie, Scout, Viper, Meiko) is one of the most-cited LPL Worlds-winning lineups, with Scout and Viper carrying the late-game pressure and the team's macro game widely studied in coaching circles.

Scene presence has dipped since the 2021 title, with the org out of recent Worlds podiums. Profile frames Worlds 2021 as the centerpiece. Also fields a Valorant roster in VCT Pacific.
