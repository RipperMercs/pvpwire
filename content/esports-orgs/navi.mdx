NAVI is one of the foundational Eastern European esports brands. Founded in Kyiv in 2009, the organization has held tier-1 rosters across Counter-Strike, Dota 2, Valorant, Apex Legends, and Rainbow Six Siege.

The two genre-defining wins anchor the org's competitive identity: The International 1 in 2011 (founding-moment Dota 2 result) and the PGL Major Stockholm CS:GO title in 2021 (the s1mple-led roster's first Major after multiple finals appearances). The latter is the more cited result in modern memory because s1mple's individual run through 2018 to 2021 ranks among the most dominant in CS history.

The current CS2 roster is in a rebuild phase post-s1mple departure. The org continues to compete at international tier-1 in Dota 2 and Valorant, with recognized rosters across the broader portfolio.
