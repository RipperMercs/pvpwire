ZETA DIVISION is Japan's flagship competitive esports organization. Founded in 2018 in Tokyo, the org has built its identity around the VCT Pacific franchise in Valorant, the Apex Legends ALGS roster, and a Street Fighter 6 presence in the FGC.

The 2022 VCT Masters Reykjavik third-place finish is the signature international Valorant result and one of the most-watched runs in Japanese esports history. ZETA's Reykjavik run drove peak Japanese-language Twitch viewership for any Valorant event.

ZETA represents the Japanese Valorant scene at international VCT events and is the recognized JP esports brand for cross-region competitive identity.
