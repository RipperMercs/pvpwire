Hanwha Life Esports, commonly referred to as HLE, is a Korean League of Legends organization founded in 2018 when Hanwha Life Insurance acquired the ROX Tigers franchise slot. The org continues the ROX Tigers historical lineage in the LCK.

HLE has been an LCK contender across 2024 to 2025 with multiple deep playoff runs and Worlds qualifications. The roster is anchored by Zeka and Viper alongside other LCK stars across recent splits.

Adds depth to the KR representation in the orgs index alongside T1, Gen.G, DRX, KT Rolster, and Dplus KIA.
