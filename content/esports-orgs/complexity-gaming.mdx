Complexity Gaming is one of the oldest still-active North American esports organizations. Founded in 2003 by Jason Lake, the org built its identity in Counter-Strike during the 1.6 era and has continuously operated CS rosters across CS:GO and now CS2.

The roster has gone through multiple cycles, with the late 2010s "Juggernaut" era as a notable rebuild stretch. Currently fielding a tier-2 to tier-1 CS2 roster with regional NA presence.

Owned by the Goff Sports group (Jerry Jones, Cowboys ownership), giving the org one of the few mainstream-sports-ownership setups in NA esports. Headquartered at the Star in Frisco, Texas.
