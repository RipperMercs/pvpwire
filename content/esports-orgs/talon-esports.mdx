Talon Esports is an APAC-rooted esports organization founded in 2018. Originally based in Hong Kong, the org is currently headquartered in Bangkok and competes across the Pacific region in Valorant, League of Legends, and Apex Legends.

The Valorant operation is anchored by a VCT Pacific franchise slot. The LoL roster competes in the Pacific Championship Series (PCS), the regional league for the broader Asia-Pacific region outside the four major LoL leagues.

Talon closes the SEA / Pacific region representation gap in the global tier-1 orgs index. Distinct from Korea-anchored brands (T1, Gen.G, DRX, KT, Hanwha Life, Dplus KIA): Talon's home is Southeast Asia, not Korea.
