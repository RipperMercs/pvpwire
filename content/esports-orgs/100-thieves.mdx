100 Thieves is a North American esports organization founded in 2017 by former Call of Duty pro Nadeshot. The org built a competitive identity in League of Legends (LCS franchise) and Valorant (VCT Americas) alongside a content and apparel brand that has scaled beyond pure esports.

The 2021 LCS Spring Championship is the org's signature LoL result. The Valorant operation has competed at international tier-1 across multiple VCT cycles, including the 2021 Masters Reykjavik final appearance.

The org scaled back several rosters in 2024, including the Call of Duty Los Angeles Thieves operation. LoL and Valorant remain the core competitive operations alongside the content arm and the apparel business.
