Heroic is a Danish CS organization founded in 2016. The org has been a top-five international CS team consistently across the 2022 to 2025 window, with the 2022 BLAST Premier World Final win and the 2023 ESL Pro League Season 17 title as the signature international results.

Single-game focus on Counter-Strike. The roster identity has been built around aggressive entry-fragger play and consistent tournament finals appearances rather than a single-dynasty stretch.

Heroic adds CS-scene depth to the orgs index alongside NAVI, Astralis, Vitality, MOUZ, and the other Danish brand presence in modern CS.
