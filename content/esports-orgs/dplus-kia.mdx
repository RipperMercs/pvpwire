Dplus KIA is a Korean League of Legends organization founded in 2017. Formerly known as DAMWON Gaming, the org won the 2020 LoL World Championship in Shanghai, ending a multi-year non-Korean dominance of the title and beginning a brief LCK return to the top.

The 2020 winning roster (Nuguri, Canyon, ShowMaker, Ghost, BeryL) is one of the most-cited LCK Worlds-winning lineups. The 2021 final loss to EDG ended the org's chance at back-to-back titles.

Multiple rebrands followed: DAMWON KIA in 2021 (Kia sponsorship), Dplus KIA in 2023 (Dplus joining as title sponsor). Current operations remain in the LCK.
