LOUD is a Brazilian esports organization founded in 2019 that scaled rapidly into a content-and-competitive brand. The 2022 VCT Champions win in Istanbul is the org's defining moment and the LATAM region's breakthrough on the international Valorant stage.

The 2022 winning roster (aspas, Less, pANcada, Sacy, saadhak) became the most-cited LATAM Valorant lineup in the title's history. aspas in particular emerged as one of the top duelists of the entire competitive scene.

Current operations span Valorant (VCT Americas), League of Legends (CBLOL), Counter-Strike 2, fighting games, and content. LOUD is among the most visible Brazilian esports brands by social audience size.
