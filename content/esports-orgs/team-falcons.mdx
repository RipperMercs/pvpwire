Team Falcons is a Saudi Arabia-based esports organization founded in 2017 in Riyadh. The org expanded rapidly across the 2023 to 2025 window into a multi-title roster spanning Counter-Strike, Apex Legends, Rocket League, Dota 2, fighting games (Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8), F1 Esports, and EA Sports FC.

The expansion was backed by Saudi capital and is closely tied to the Esports World Cup ecosystem (also Saudi-funded). Falcons rosters have competed at the EWC across multiple titles since the event launched.

The Saudi backing is a documented part of the org's competitive identity. Profile notes the EWC tie and Saudi sponsorship factually, matching the editorial framing decided for the EWC tournament entry. The competitive results across the multi-title portfolio have been mixed-to-strong, with notable Apex and Rocket League runs and Dota 2 international placements.
