T1, originally SK Telecom T1, is the most decorated organization in League of Legends history. The four World Championship wins (2013, 2015, 2016, 2023) and a roster anchored by Faker, who is widely considered the greatest competitive LoL player of all time, place T1 in a category of one within LoL pro history.

The 2023 Worlds run in Seoul was the storybook conclusion to a multi-year competitive gap and reaffirmed Faker's longevity at the highest level. The org has since expanded operations into Valorant (VCT Pacific franchise), Dota 2, and Apex Legends, with rosters that compete at international tier-1 in each title.

The T1 organizational identity is also tied to the SK Telecom telecom-rivalry era of LCK with KT Rolster, the dominant rivalry of the LoL pro scene's first decade.
