Cloud9 is the most consistent multi-game North American esports organization. Founded in 2013 in Los Angeles, the org has fielded competitive rosters across Counter-Strike, League of Legends, Valorant, Apex Legends, Rocket League, and most other titles with significant pro infrastructure.

The 2018 ELEAGUE Major Boston win in CS:GO is the org's most-cited result and remains the only North American CS Major win in the title's history. The LoL operations have produced multiple LCS Championships across the franchise era, with the Cloud9 brand being the longest-running NA LoL identity alongside TSM.

Current operations span every major scene the NA audience cares about, with rosters in active rebuild cycles across CS2 and Valorant in 2025 to 2026.
