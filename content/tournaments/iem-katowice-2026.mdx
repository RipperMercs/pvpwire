IEM Katowice is the traditional first major Counter-Strike event of the calendar year. The 2026 edition runs January 29 to February 8 at the Spodek Arena in Katowice, Poland, the same venue that has hosted the event since the early CS:GO era.

The Spodek setting is one of the most-cited live atmospheres in competitive CS, with the venue's bowl shape producing crowd noise that the broadcasts have made part of the IEM Katowice identity. The event regularly draws the largest CS live audience of the year outside of the Majors.

Prize pool of $1M USD with the standard ESL format: opening Swiss stage, single-elimination playoffs in the arena. Top tier-1 CS rosters worldwide attend; the event seeds heavily into the Intel Grand Slam and ESL Pro Tour standings for the rest of the year.
