The Six Invitational is Rainbow Six Siege's annual flagship event and the genre-defining tournament for the title. Run by Ubisoft directly, the event has been the year-opener for the R6 competitive calendar since 2017.

The 2026 edition runs February 9 to 22 in Sao Paulo, Brazil, with a $3M+ prize pool. The Six Invitational format includes a group stage followed by a double-elimination playoff bracket, with the LAN finals drawing the largest R6 live crowds of the year.

Brazil hosting reflects the dominant role of the Brazilian R6 scene in the global competitive structure: the LATAM region has been one of the most-cited regional anchors of competitive R6 since the title's launch.
