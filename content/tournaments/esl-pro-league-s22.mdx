ESL Pro League is the year-round Counter-Strike league competition that anchors the tier-1 CS calendar between Majors. The league runs twice per year (Season 22 in spring, Season 23 in fall) with a round-robin group stage followed by single-elimination playoffs at the ESL studio.

Season 22 runs May 27 to June 21 in Stockholm, with all teams across the standard 32-team format. The league seeds heavily into the Intel Grand Slam standings and the broader ESL Pro Tour ranking.

ESL Pro League is more important for tier-1 CS rosters than a single tournament might suggest: the league's continuous competition pattern is what shapes mid-season form across the international CS calendar.
