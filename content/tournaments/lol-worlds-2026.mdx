The League of Legends World Championship, commonly referred to as Worlds, is the annual flagship event for competitive LoL and the single most-watched esports event of the year by viewership.

The 2026 edition runs October 10 to November 8 with the host region to be confirmed. Worlds rotates between Asia, Europe, and North America on a multi-year cycle. The format includes the Play-In stage, the Swiss-style Group Stage, single-elimination Knockouts, and the Grand Finals at the host arena.

Riot has run Worlds since 2011, and the event consistently produces the largest concurrent viewership numbers in esports. The 2024 Worlds final between T1 and BLG drew peaks well above 6M concurrent viewers (excluding CN audiences).
