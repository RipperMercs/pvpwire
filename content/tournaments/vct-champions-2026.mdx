VCT Champions is the year-end Valorant championship and the calendar's defining Valorant event. Run by Riot Games since 2021, Champions brings together the top teams from the four regional VCT leagues (Americas, EMEA, Pacific, China) for the annual world title.

The 2026 edition runs October 15 to November 8 with the host city to be confirmed. Format is the standard Champions structure: group stage with double-elimination playoff bracket, with the LAN finals as the closing event.

Riot has standardized the VCT format since 2023 with the franchise league restructuring. Champions has produced multiple memorable runs across its short history (LOUD Istanbul 2022, EG Los Angeles 2023, Gen.G Seoul 2024).
