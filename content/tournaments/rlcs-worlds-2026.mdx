The Rocket League Championship Series World Championship, commonly referred to as RLCS Worlds, is the annual year-end event for competitive Rocket League. Run by Psyonix and Epic Games, RLCS Worlds is the calendar tentpole that closes the competitive season.

The 2026 edition runs August 19 to 30 with the host city to be confirmed. Format is double-elimination playoffs with regional qualifiers seeding teams from EU, NA, OCE, SAM, MENA, and APAC.

RLCS is one of the few competitive scenes outside the FGC where individual play translates as cleanly to broadcast viewing as to player-base experience: the game's format makes spectator highlights legible to viewers who do not play.
