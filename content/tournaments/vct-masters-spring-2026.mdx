VCT Masters is the mid-season Valorant international tentpole. Run by Riot Games, the event slots between the regional VCT split playoffs (Americas, EMEA, Pacific, China) and the year-end VCT Champions, giving Valorant two international events per year.

The Spring 2026 edition runs April 15 to May 3, with the host city to be confirmed. Format is double-elimination playoffs with seeding from the regional VCT splits.

Riot has run Masters annually since 2021, and the event has produced multiple memorable runs (Sentinels Reykjavik 2021, Paper Rex Madrid 2024, Fnatic Tokyo 2023). The 2026 edition continues the established calendar position.
