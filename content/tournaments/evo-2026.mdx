EVO, short for Evolution Championship Series, is the annual fighting-game championship and the single calendar event that holds the entire FGC together. Held in Las Vegas every August, the event is unique among major esports tournaments in that it covers multiple titles in parallel rather than a single game.

The 2026 edition runs August 7 to 9 at the Las Vegas Convention Center. The standard multi-title lineup includes Street Fighter 6, Tekken 8, Guilty Gear Strive, Mortal Kombat 1, Super Smash Bros Ultimate, and Dragon Ball FighterZ, with additional titles rotating in based on community demand and competitive scene size.

EVO uses the FGC-standard double-elimination bracket format with open-bracket entry. The Sunday finals at Las Vegas Convention Center are one of the most-watched FGC events of the year. Title-by-title prize pools vary; the listed total is the aggregated event-wide prize pool.
