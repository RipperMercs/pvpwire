The Champions Chess Tour, commonly referred to as CCT, is a multi-event competitive chess season run by Chess.com across the calendar year. The 2026 edition runs February through December with a series of monthly online events building to a year-end LAN final.

Division I features Magnus Carlsen, Hikaru Nakamura, Fabiano Caruana, and the rest of the world top 10. Lower divisions feature additional grandmasters and titled players, with promotion and relegation between divisions across the season.

The tournament structure is rapid and blitz time controls (faster than classical chess), which makes the format more televisable than long-form classical events. Chess.com has been the dominant operator of the modern competitive chess streaming era; CCT is the calendar anchor for that infrastructure.

Carlsen joined Team Liquid Chess in March 2024, the first chess vertical for a tier-1 traditional esports organization. The CCT presence ties directly to that partnership.
