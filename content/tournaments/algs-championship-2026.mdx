The Apex Legends Global Series Championship, commonly referred to as ALGS Champs, is the year-end finals of the ALGS season. Run by Electronic Arts and Respawn, ALGS is one of the few battle royale esports formats that reads on a calendar with consistent year-over-year structure.

The 2026 Championship runs November 12 to 22 with the host city to be confirmed. Format includes a group stage with point-accumulation across multiple matches, then a Match Point bracket for the final tournament structure unique to ALGS.

The point-accumulation Match Point format is unusual among esports tournament structures: a team wins by both reaching the score threshold and winning the next match after reaching it. The structure produces dramatic late-tournament swings that have given ALGS some of the most-watched BR moments of the past few years.
