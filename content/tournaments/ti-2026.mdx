The International, commonly referred to as TI, is Dota 2's annual flagship competitive event. Run by Valve since 2011, TI has been the defining event of the Dota 2 competitive year for the entire history of the modern title.

The 2026 edition runs September 15 to October 4 with the host city to be confirmed. Format is the standard TI structure: open and regional qualifiers feeding into a group stage at the host venue, then double-elimination playoffs with the grand finals as the closing event.

TI's prize pool has declined significantly since Valve discontinued the Battle Pass community-funding model that previously inflated prize pools to record levels (the 2021 TI prize pool exceeded $40M). The current TI is closer to the prize-pool size of other tier-1 multi-game events. The competitive importance to the Dota 2 scene remains unchanged.
