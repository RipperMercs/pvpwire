PlanetSide proved in 2003 that hundreds of simultaneous players could engage in coordinated FPS combat across persistent maps. The three-faction structure (Vanu Sovereignty, New Conglomerate, Terran Republic), the continent capture meta, and the combined-arms vehicle system were all firsts at this scale. The technical achievement was significant. The audience that experienced peak PlanetSide remembers the continent-spanning fights as something subsequent MMOFPS games have struggled to match. The original sunset in 2016. PlanetSide 2 carries the format forward.
