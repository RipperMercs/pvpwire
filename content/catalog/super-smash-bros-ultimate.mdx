Super Smash Bros Ultimate is the platform fighter against which all others are measured. The competitive scene runs almost entirely on community-built infrastructure with grassroots tournaments rather than publisher circuits. The roster size, the matchup chart, and the ledge-trapping meta all reward years of investment.
