Albion Online is the rare full-loot MMO that prioritized accessibility without diluting the threat model. Loadouts are classless and gear-defined, which means a roster's win conditions can change between fights. The crystal league GvG bracket is structured like a competitive ladder, and the top brackets attract serious organized play.
