Clash Royale launched in 2016 and became one of the defining mobile PvP titles of its era. The real-time card play, the three-minute match length, and the elixir economy produced a format that translated well to competitive play. The Clash Royale League circuit has run continuously, with Path of Legends as the modern matchmaking ladder and the World Finals delivering one of the largest prize pools in mobile esports.
