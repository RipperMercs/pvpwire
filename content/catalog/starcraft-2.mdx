StarCraft 2 is the genre-defining 1v1 RTS, still operating a meaningful pro circuit through GSL and ESL Pro Tour. Korean dominance has continued for the entire life of the title, and the format remains one of the few esports where mechanical skill, build order theory, and reaction-driven decision making all matter equally.
