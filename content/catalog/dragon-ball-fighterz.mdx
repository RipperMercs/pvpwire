Dragon Ball FighterZ launched in 2018 with the highest-quality anime fighter animation Arc System Works had shipped to date. The 3v3 tag format, the assist mechanics, and the Vanish system produce a fighting game that rewards both individual character mastery and team synergy. The Dragon Ball FighterZ World Tour has run continuously since launch, making this one of the most stably supported anime fighter pro circuits.
