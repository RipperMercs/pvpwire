Guilty Gear Strive is the entry that finally widened the anime-fighter funnel without losing its identity. The wall-break system gave every match a structural rhythm, the Roman Cancel mechanic stayed deep enough to reward serious study, and the floor-based ranked progression encourages players to keep climbing instead of bouncing off MMR walls.
