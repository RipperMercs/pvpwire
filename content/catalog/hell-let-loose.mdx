Hell Let Loose released 1.0 in 2021 with a 50v50 WWII milsim format that relies on resource management, role specialization (officer, support, anti-tank, recon, armor), and meaningful logistics. The audience that came for the milsim seriousness has stayed. The console release expanded the player base meaningfully beyond the PC milsim cohort.
