Mechabellum is the auto-battler that took the format back to its 1v1 roots. Unit positioning, tech tree decisions, and counter picks dominate over the deck-building randomness that defines most other auto-battlers. The competitive scene is small but high quality, with a community-organized tournament cadence that the developer has supported.
