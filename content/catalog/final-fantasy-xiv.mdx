Final Fantasy XIV is one of the largest MMOs in the world, and most of its audience plays for the PvE story. The PvP layer, especially Crystalline Conflict, has cultivated a smaller but committed competitive scene with seasonal ladders and community tournaments. The fact that the publisher does not lean into the competitive identity is part of why the players who do treat it that way are uniquely earnest.
