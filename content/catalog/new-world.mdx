New World launched messy and is, several years later, one of the more interesting modern MMO PvP experiences for organized play. The 50v50 territory war system is the centerpiece. Movement, weapon swapping, and coordinated heal logistics actually decide outcomes, which means a disciplined company can outpunch a larger one.
