Pax Dei is positioned as a social MMO first and a PvP MMO second, which has produced a population that loves the world and is suspicious of anyone trying to militarize it. The contested zones and heartlands give organized players room to operate, and the long-term arc of the project will be decided by whether the sandbox systems harden enough to support actual conflict.
