Hearthstone broke the CCG audience open by tying digital design tightly to streaming and mobile play. The pro scene has cooled significantly since the franchise's peak, but the ranked ladder remains a defining experience and the Battlegrounds mode opened a second wind for players who wanted competitive depth without deck-building burden.
