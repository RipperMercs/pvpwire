Pokemon Unite launched in 2021 as a mobile-first 5v5 MOBA built around ten-minute matches and the Pokemon roster. TiMi's design pairs the genre's lane-and-objective structure with the franchise's accessible audience, and the inclusion in the Pokemon World Championships gives Unite a competitive circuit that traditional mobile MOBAs (outside of MLBB) have struggled to maintain.
