World of Warcraft's competitive PvP scene is built almost entirely around the 3v3 arena, with rated battlegrounds and the newer Solo Shuffle and Blitz modes serving as feeder ladders. The AWC circuit has run continuously for over a decade, and the gladiator title remains one of the most prestigious season rewards in any MMO.
