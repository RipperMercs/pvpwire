Hunt: Showdown is the extraction shooter that prioritizes sound design and deliberate pacing over the high-tempo gunplay of its peers. The bayou setting, the bounty token mechanic, and the inheritance of perks from dead hunters create a loop that rewards patience and punishes reflexive engagement.
