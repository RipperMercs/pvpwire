Marvel Rivals stepped into the hero shooter audience that Overwatch League's collapse left behind. The 6v6 format, the team-up abilities, and the Marvel character pool drew a large initial population. The competitive scene is rebuilding the third-party tournament infrastructure that hero shooters need before any league can stabilize.
