Ragnarok Online launched in 2002 and produced one of the most enduring siege PvP formats in the genre. War of Emperium, the weekly two-hour scheduled castle siege, became a competitive format that ran continuously across official and private server populations for over two decades. The job system, the precast mechanics, and the breaking-the-emperium objective are all reference points for any subsequent MMO that ships scheduled siege content. The game remains operational with a global private server scene that continues to refine the WoE meta.
