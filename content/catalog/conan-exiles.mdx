Conan Exiles launched in 2018 and has continued operating as one of the more durable survival sandboxes. The PvP-server population, anchored around scheduled raid windows and the deep base-building system, has produced a community that takes the political layer seriously. Private server hosting has been particularly strong and continues to drive much of the game's PvP activity.
