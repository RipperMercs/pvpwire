Age of Conan launched in 2008 with one of the most innovative melee combat systems in any MMO and a mature-rated tone that gave the PvP servers a distinctively brutal culture. The FFA PvP zones, the city sieges, and the 24/7 conflict on the dedicated PvP servers produced a community that has stayed loyal across more than fifteen years of operation. The game continues to operate on a reduced server footprint, with the PvP audience smaller but committed.
