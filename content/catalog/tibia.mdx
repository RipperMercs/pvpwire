Tibia launched in 1997 and has been continuously operational since, an unusual achievement for any MMO. The top-down 2D presentation hides one of the more punishing PvP systems in the genre, with full loot drops on death on hardcore servers and a community that has been refining its political map for over twenty-five years. Tibia is the canonical example of an MMO that outlasts its design era through community gravity rather than novelty.
