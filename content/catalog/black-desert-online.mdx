Black Desert Online sits at the harsh end of the gear-gap spectrum. The conquest war meta rewards macro coordination, terrain control, and the kind of attendance discipline that smaller modern guilds can't sustain. Top guilds in BDO often have rosters that span a decade of MMO PvP history.
