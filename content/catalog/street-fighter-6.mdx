Street Fighter 6 reset the fighting game discourse. The Drive system replaced the traditional super meter with a unified offense-defense resource that gives every match a built-in tempo. Modern controls opened the door to players who would have bounced off classical execution, and the master rating ladder gave the top of the ranked scene a tournament-grade benchmark.
