Dark and Darker is the fantasy answer to the extraction shooter wave. Melee-first combat in dungeon environments with class-based loadouts (fighter, ranger, cleric, rogue, wizard, barbarian, bard, warlock), full loot on death, and a high-stakes economy. The early access has been turbulent, the audience has stayed, and the format proves that extraction-style design can live outside the realistic-modern-military template Tarkov defined.
