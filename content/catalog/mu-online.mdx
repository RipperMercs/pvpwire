Mu Online released in 2001 as one of the formative Korean MMOs of the early 2000s era. The isometric presentation, the castle siege format, and the gear-driven PvP loop made the game massively popular in Southeast Asia and Latin America in particular, where the audience has stayed across more than two decades of operation. Numerous private servers continue to run alongside the official live service.
