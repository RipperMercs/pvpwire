City of Heroes launched in 2004 with one of the most beloved character creators in any MMO and a PvP scene that grew unexpectedly serious through the Arena format and the hero-versus-villain Recluse's Victory zone. The 2012 sunset was an iconic loss in the community memory. The 2019 community-server resurrection, including Homecoming, has kept the game alive with active development for nearly six years now, an unusual second life for a sunset MMO.
