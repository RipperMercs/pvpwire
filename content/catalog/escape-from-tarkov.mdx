Escape from Tarkov is the cultural source of the modern extraction shooter wave. The realistic ballistics, the body-part hit zones, the secure container economy, and the raid-extract loop are all systems that subsequent extraction games iterate on. Tarkov's audience is older, more committed, and more willing to absorb friction than the BR audience that often gets compared to it.
