Guild Wars 2 has two distinct PvP cultures sharing one client. Structured PvP is a 5v5 conquest format with automated tournaments and a competitive ladder. World versus World is the persistent-server large-scale system that runs week-long matchups between three worlds. The audience that plays GW2 PvP today is small, knowledgeable, and uniquely tolerant of an MMO that does not chase trends.
