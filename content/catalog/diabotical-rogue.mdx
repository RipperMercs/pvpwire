Diabotical Rogue takes the arena FPS movement and weapon model and grafts it onto a hybrid extraction-BR format. The result is a smaller-audience experiment with serious commitment from the arena FPS community. Whether the format finds an audience or whether the project pivots back to pure arena modes is the open question for the title's future.
