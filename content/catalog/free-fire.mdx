Free Fire launched in 2017 as a mobile-first battle royale optimized for low-end devices and ten-minute matches. The audience that came was massive in Latin America, India, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East, with the Free Fire World Series consistently producing some of the largest live audiences in any esport. The Western press systematically underreports Free Fire's scale relative to its actual cultural footprint outside North America and Europe.
