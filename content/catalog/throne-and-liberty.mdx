Throne and Liberty is the current-era home of choice for a wave of MMO PvP guilds that needed somewhere to land after Lost Ark and New World cycled. The siege loop is the draw. Conflict zones, boontide events, and castle sieges create an active weekly rhythm of large-scale engagements, and the weapon-swap system gives roster composition real weight.
