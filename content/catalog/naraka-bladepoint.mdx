Naraka: Bladepoint launched in 2021 with a 60-player battle royale built around melee combat, grappling movement, and a deep parry system. The Asian competitive scene has produced a real pro circuit with the Naraka World Championship. The combat depth and the verticality have given the game a distinct identity in a BR market dominated by gun-first gameplay.
