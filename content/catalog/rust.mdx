Rust is the modern sandbox PvP success story. The monthly wipe cycle keeps the game's economy from stratifying, and the base-raid endgame produces some of the highest-stakes engagements in any survival game. Streamer-organized events brought Rust into the broader competitive conversation and gave organized clans a reason to operate at scale.
