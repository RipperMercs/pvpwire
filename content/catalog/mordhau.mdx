Mordhau launched in 2019 from Triternion as the Chivalry-lineage medieval melee game with a deeper directional swing system. The free-aim mouse control over swing arcs (chambers, morphs, accelerated and decelerated drags, feints) creates a higher mechanical skill ceiling than Chivalry, which is the entire reason the duel-server community migrated.

The Frontline mode runs large team objective combat, but the competitive identity is the duel server scene: dedicated 1v1 servers running ladder etiquette, with the duel community essentially being the Chivalry 1 duelists who wanted more depth. Tournaments are player-organized rather than developer-supported.

Mordhau represents the "split" in the medieval melee category: the casual / cinematic siege audience went to Chivalry 2, the duel-purist mechanical-skill audience stayed in Mordhau.
