Meridian 59 is the source. Released in 1996, it was the first commercially launched graphical MMO and shipped with full open-world PvP, factional combat, and the political-economic dynamics that subsequent MMOs would spend the next two decades iterating on. The audience was small by modern standards. The influence on every PvP MMO designer who came after is nearly total.
