Marvel Snap launched in 2022 from Second Dinner, founded by ex-Hearthstone designers including Ben Brode. The three-minute match format, the unique double-down "snap" mechanic, and the rapid card release cadence have produced one of the most active mobile CCG audiences. The Conquest mode, added in 2023, pushed the game toward more structured competitive play, with the Infinity Tournament circuit emerging as the format's pro tier.
