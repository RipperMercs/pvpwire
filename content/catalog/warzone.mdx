Warzone leveraged the CoD engine and audience to become the largest free-to-play BR by player count. The Gulag, the loadout drop, and the buyback economy gave it a unique decision tree against PUBG and Apex. The annual cycle is a feature for the audience and a problem for long-term meta study.
