Brawlhalla launched in 2017 as a free-to-play platform fighter with day-one cross-play across all platforms. The tournament circuit (Brawlhalla World Championship plus regional events) has paid serious money to top players for years. The mobile release expanded the audience significantly. As a Smash alternative for players who cannot or will not engage with Nintendo's hardware ecosystem, Brawlhalla has been the genre's most consistent answer.
