Knight Online launched in 2002 with a two-faction war structure (Karus versus El Morad) that drove relentless open-world PvP across contested zones. The audience that stayed came primarily from Turkey, Korea, and Latin America, with the Turkish private server scene producing some of the most active classic MMO PvP communities through the 2010s. The official service continues to operate.
