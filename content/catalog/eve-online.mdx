EVE Online is the longest-running PvP sandbox in the genre, and the only major MMO that has stayed on a single shard for its entire life. The political layer is the actual game. Wars are fought with spies, propaganda campaigns, market sabotage, and forty-thousand-player set-piece fleet engagements that occasionally make mainstream news.

The catalog of EVE guilds maps cleanly to the history of bloc warfare. Goonswarm Federation, Pandemic Legion, Test Alliance Please Ignore, and Northern Coalition are the four anchor entries, with shifting alliances and grudges that span fifteen-plus years.
