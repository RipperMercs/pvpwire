iRacing is sim racing's gold standard. The license system, the iRating ladder, and the official series structure produce a competitive environment that more closely resembles real motorsport than any other competitive game. Pro drivers run iRacing seasons. Amateur drivers can compete in series with multi-season standings. The infrastructure is unmatched in the racing genre.
