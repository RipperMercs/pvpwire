Chivalry 2 scaled the original up to 64 players and traded the duel-purist crowd for a more cinematic siege experience. The four base classes (Knight, Vanguard, Footman, Archer) each branch into multiple subclasses, and the Team Objective mode runs through narrative siege scenarios with attacker and defender phases. The competitive 1v1 community has stayed smaller than the Mordhau scene that splintered off after Chivalry 1, but the broader player base made Chiv 2 the default modern entry point for the medieval melee category.
