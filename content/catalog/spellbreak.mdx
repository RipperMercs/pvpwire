Spellbreak launched in 2020 from Proletariat as a battle royale built around six magic gauntlets (fire, ice, lightning, stone, toxic, wind) with elemental-combo combat (fire on toxic creates flamewall, ice on lightning slows and conducts, etc.) The traversal system used runes for flight, levitation, teleportation, and short blinks, giving the game a distinct movement language.

Sunset January 2023 after Microsoft acquired Proletariat to fold the studio into the World of Warcraft team. The closure was widely cited as one of the more genuinely different BRs of the genre's saturation era getting killed for unrelated business reasons rather than for player decline.

Profile preserved in the catalog as historical context for what the BR genre could have looked like beyond the Fortnite / Apex / PUBG / Warzone template.
