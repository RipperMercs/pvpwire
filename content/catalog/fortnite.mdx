Fortnite is two games sharing a client. Build mode is a movement and structure puzzle that rewards thousands of hours of muscle memory. Zero Build is the audience-friendly version that opened up the competitive funnel. FNCS prize pools are among the largest in any esport, with a tournament structure that keeps the top of the ladder economically viable for amateurs and pros alike.
