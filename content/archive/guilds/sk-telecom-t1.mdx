T1, originally SK Telecom T1, is the most decorated organization in League of Legends history. Four World Championship wins (2013, 2015, 2016, 2023) and a roster anchored by Faker, who is widely considered one of the greatest competitive players in any esport, place T1 in a category of one within LoL pro history.

The organization has expanded operations into Valorant, Dota 2, and other titles, but the LoL identity remains the central pillar of T1's competitive legacy.
