JARBO was one of the formative Asheron's Call Darktide organizations, with a dominance period spanning the first several years of the server's history. The guild operated as a high-population monarchy with influence over the core Darktide leveling zones during the era when zone control was effectively progression control.

The JARBO leadership produced a roster that subsequently appeared across DAoC and EVE in identifiable clusters, though no successor organization carried the name forward.
