Northern Coalition is one of the recognized elite EVE Online alliances, with an operational history that overlaps significantly with Pandemic Legion through the N3 coalition era. The alliance's capital warfare doctrines contributed to the era when supercapital fleet operations defined high-end EVE engagement.

Northern Coalition's role in the Bloodbath of B-R5RB in 2014 placed it at the center of one of the most cited single events in MMO political history.
