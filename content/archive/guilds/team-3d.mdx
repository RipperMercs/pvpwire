Team 3D was one of the dominant North American Counter-Strike organizations during the early professional CS era. The roster produced results across the CPL circuit and the original esports league structures, with the organization treated as a benchmark for North American competitive Counter-Strike during the period when the game's professional infrastructure was being built.
