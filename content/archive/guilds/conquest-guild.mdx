Conquest is a multi-game competitive guild that operated a public website at conquest-guild.com and is documented in TheMercs' own navigation sidebar (archived 2012). The Conquest name appears across multiple PvP MMO communities of the era, though the specific lineage and game-by-game operational record is pending fuller research.

Per TheMercs' own published doctrine, contemporaries listed in their navigation were rivals or fellow-elite competitors, not allies. The relationship with TheMercs was adversarial.

Editorial note: this profile is not to be confused with Conquest (Merlin), the DAoC realm guild, which has a separate profile.
