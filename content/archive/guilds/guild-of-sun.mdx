Guild of Sun (SUN) was one of the dominant Darkfall Online alliance cores, operating across both the original Darkfall and Darkfall Unholy Wars eras with sustained city control and naval presence. The organizational continuity across the title transition was unusual in a genre where most alliances dissolve at population breakpoints.

The Guild of Sun lineage continues into the modern full-loot revival scene, including operations in Mortal Online 2 and adjacent niche projects. This is one of the clearest examples of a single organization carrying identity across multiple games in the genre.
