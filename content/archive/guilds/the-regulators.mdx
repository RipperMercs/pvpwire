The Regulators is a competitive guild that operated a public website at theregulators.org and is documented in TheMercs' own navigation sidebar (archived 2012). Specific game associations and operational dates are pending fuller research; the contemporary listing places the guild in the late-1990s through early-2010s active-PvP era.

Per TheMercs' own published doctrine, contemporaries listed in their navigation were rivals or fellow-elite competitors, not allies. The relationship with TheMercs was adversarial.
