Pandemic Legion is one of the most cited names in EVE Online's elite alliance lineage, with an operational history that spans nearly the entire modern era of the game. The alliance is associated with elite small to medium fleet doctrines and capital warfare leadership.

The Bloodbath of B-R5RB in 2014 placed Pandemic Legion at the center of mainstream gaming press coverage of EVE for the dollar value of destroyed assets in a single engagement. Beyond that single event, the alliance's broader contribution to EVE political history is the role it has played as a benchmark for organized fleet operations against numerically larger but less coordinated opponents.
