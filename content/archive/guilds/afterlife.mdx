Afterlife operated as one of the top tier EverQuest raid guilds during the height of the Plane of Time era. The guild's progression race results placed it in the cited canon of EQ raid history alongside Fires of Heaven and the small group of organizations that defined what top-tier MMO raid leadership looked like in the early 2000s.
