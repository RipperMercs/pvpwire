PaxLair is one of the longest continuously running player organizations in any MMO. Founded in the late 1990s as a player city within Ultima Online, PaxLair has maintained a community presence across the full lifetime of the game and across multiple shards. The organization is a cultural anchor for the broader UO community and a frequently cited example of player-driven persistent identity in the genre.
