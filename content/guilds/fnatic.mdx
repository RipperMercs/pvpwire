Fnatic is one of the longest tenured esports organizations in continuous operation. Founded in 2004, the organization has fielded competitive rosters across Counter-Strike, League of Legends, Dota 2, Valorant, and most other titles with significant pro infrastructure.

The 2011 League of Legends Season 1 World Championship established Fnatic at the founding of professional LoL. The organization's CS:GO rosters during the mid-2010s competitive peak produced multiple major event wins, and the modern Valorant and LoL operations continue the multi-title organizational identity.
