Combine operated on Merlin as one of the Hibernia cores during the height of DAoC's three-realm RvR. The guild's operational tempo contributed to Hibernia's ability to defend frontier-zone presence against Albion and Midgard pressure during the period when nightly attendance was the practical determinant of realm map control.
