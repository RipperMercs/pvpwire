Fires of Heaven is one of the most cited guild names in MMO history. Founded in 1999 in EverQuest under the leadership of Furor, the guild operated as a top tier raid guild during a period when the public forum culture around raid progression was its own form of competitive theater. The Fires of Heaven boards became one of the most read MMO community spaces of the early 2000s.

The guild carried organizational identity into World of Warcraft and continued operations across multiple raid expansions. The continuity arc, a single guild leadership group carrying brand identity across more than two decades, is unusual in the genre and is a frequently cited example of sustained guild culture done right.
