Test Alliance Please Ignore (TEST) is one of the major EVE Online alliances, with a sustained operational presence across more than a decade of bloc warfare. The alliance grew from origins on the Reddit gaming community and developed into a coalition-level political actor.

The Fountain War in 2013 and World War Bee 2 starting in 2020 are the two most cited narrative arcs in the alliance's history. World War Bee 2, fought primarily against Goonswarm and the Imperium, became one of the most extensively documented MMO wars in any genre.
